THE LOCAL’S CHOICE : THE EXPERTISE OF FOUR GENERATIONS

In the 1880s, N.B. Laughlin arrived in Santa Fe on horseback. In the early 1900s, he served as Supreme Court Judge and quickly established himself in the community. His grandson, Laughlin Barker, filled Santa Fe’s need for real estate expertise by starting his practice in 1964. His son, David Barker, was raised in the business. He and his wife Lisa purchased Barker Realty from Laughlin in 1985. David Barker’s knowledge of Santa Fe’s properties and his understanding of it’s people cannot be matched. There is no separating this level of 4th generation real estate expertise from the scope of his family’s history. Barker Realty is a Santa Fe tradition and the singular Local’s Choice.

Laughlin Barker’s first office was located on the corner of West San Francisco & Don Gaspar in a building built by N.B. Laughlin in the 1900’s
